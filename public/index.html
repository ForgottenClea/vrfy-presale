FAQ

Common questions about the VRFY presale

VRFY is the native utility token of the OracleChains network. It powers real-world asset verification including property valuations, ownership records, compliance checks, and authentication services - all verified on-chain through our decentralized validator network.

VRFY is deployed on Base network (Coinbase's L2). This provides fast transactions, low gas fees, and seamless integration with the broader Ethereum ecosystem.

Tokens will be claimable immediately after the presale concludes and liquidity is added. You'll receive a notification when claiming is available. There is no vesting period for presale participants.

The presale has 5 stages. Each stage increases the price by 100%. Stage 1 is $0.001, Stage 2 is $0.002, Stage 3 will be $0.003, and so on. Early participants get the best price.

Minimum purchase is $10 USD equivalent. There is no maximum - you can participate with any amount above the minimum.

VRFY tokens are used to: 1) Pay for oracle queries (RWA verification), 2) Stake as a validator to earn rewards, 3) Participate in governance, 4) Access premium verification services.
